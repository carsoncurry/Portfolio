About Me

Welcome to my professional portfolio page. I am a writer, designer and web developer currently navigating the wide world of full-stack website development. On this page you can find a number of projects I have developed or worked in my educational and professional careers. The skills I most pride myself on are clean and vibrant front-end design, simple user interface, mobile functionality, search engine optimization and a consistent tone of voice in all content written for the web.

I attended the University of Kansas between 2008 and 2012, where I graduated with a B.A. in English, emphasizing in creative writing. I returned to KU from 2018 - 2019 to complete the Full-Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp, where I honed my skills as a web designer. If you are interested in contacting me regarding your website (either in management or design), please don't hesitate to contact me or visit one of my social links listed below.

Selected Projects

American Safari (Group Project)

Group project utilizing front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and APIs (iNaturalist, Google Places, CORS Anywhere) to create an animal-finding travel app.

Virtual Elephant (Group Project)

Group project utilizing front and back-end technologies (Node.js, MySQL, Handlebars) and plugins (Socket.io) to create a gift giving app for cross-country use.

SuperSquad Sorter

Individual project utilizing front and back-end technologies (JQuery, Express, Node.js) to match a user's answers to the superhero squad to which they most likely belong.

The Great Station

Individual project utilizing front and back-end technologies (Moment.js, Firebase Database) to create an app that takes in trains arriving at and leaving the station and tracks their schedule.

Jurassic Trivia

Individual project utilizing front-end technologies (JavaScript, JQuery) to create a trivia game that tracks user answers while providing a timer.
